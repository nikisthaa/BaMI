BaMI: Berry and Mushroom Identification

ABOUT OUR PRODUCT

Our Product

Our product is an AI and Computer Vision program that classifies mushrooms and berries. Apart from classification, our program will also assess if it is edible or not. Users simply need to upload images of mushrooms or berries to get these results.

Passion

Our passion is helping the people of the world to enjoy hiking and stay healthy while doing it!

Motivation

We were motivated to create our product because we wanted to enhance everyone's hiking and foraging experience. We wanted our users to be able to stay safe while enjoying food they foraged in the wild.
